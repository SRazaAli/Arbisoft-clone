Making edX tracking logs Caliper compliant

The University of California San Diego (UCSD) found that the data collected from 3.4 million students enrolled in 90 edX courses was not compatible with their current analytics applications. In order to find a solution to use their data effectively, the university partnered with Arbisoft to create Open edX Caliper Feed feature, a data solution that allows for the real-time collection of course activity to flow into an analytics tool.
